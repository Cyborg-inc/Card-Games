War

What you need


  • a deck of cards
  • a flat surface to play on
  • someone to play against

How to play

Deal the deck equally among two players

Place the card face down in front of the player

Both players turn over the cards and the one with the higher value wins and the player takes both cards

If both players turn over cards that have the same value then "war" is declared

The card currently played stay there then three cards are played on top face down and a final card is played face up

The highest valued card of the two face up gets all 10 cards

The loser is the first person to run out of cards
